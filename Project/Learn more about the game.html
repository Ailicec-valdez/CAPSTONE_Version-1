About the game

What this game is all about...

'The Unknown' is an ecological survival game following a Mexican Free-tailed Bat as you help it navigate the dangers it faces in its environment. Much of the bat population today deals with several environmental factors that threaten their species survival. This game was created to raise awareness about the importance of bats and other misunderstood animals, and to educate players about the challenges they face in their natural habitats.

What can you do in the game?

Learn

Enjoy education through interative story telling as we navigate several gaming strategies. Each level focuses on different issues our bat populations are facing, this includes decrease in population due to wind turbines, climate change, human effects, ect.

Play

Enjoy several levels of gameplay as you put yourself in the shoes of a Mexican Free-tailed bat. Test your skills and see if you can survive the challenges of the world. Good Luck!

Inspire

You are the future. We hope this game inspires you to take action and make a difference for bats and other misunderstood animals. Whether it's through supporting conservation efforts, spreading awareness, or simply learning more about these incredible creatures, every action counts.
